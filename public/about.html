about us

more detail

our project is built using svelte, an innovative tool for building fast web applications with less boilerplate. we harness the reactivity of svelte combined with the robustness of typescript, enabling us to offer a reliable and user-friendly experience.

we have integrated i18n support to make our application accessible globally, and our commitment to developer experience is reflected in the use of vite and vitest for an optimized development and testing workflow.

our interface is styled with tailwind css, ensuring a responsive and modern design that adapts to all devices, enhancing the overall user interaction and accessibility.
